Welcome to Truskavets - the most beautiful place in the world

Why you should visit Truskavets:

Mineral springs

Truskavets is famous for its mineral springs, which have made it one of Ukraine's great resorts. For most visitors the primary goal is consuming the various 'local waters.' The most famous is the naphtha and sulfur-scented, slightly saline 'Naftusia.'

Nature

The town lies in an attractive little valley in the Carpathian foothills. Here you can enjoy beautiful nature of the West of Ukraine, hiking and skiing.
